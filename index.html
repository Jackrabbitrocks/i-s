Our portfolio of services to help you achieve the best smile possible

Porcelain veneers

Veneers are porcelain coverings that are bonded to the front of your teeth to look and act like natural tooth structure. They are a popular cosmetic procedure and can reverse a wide variety of smile problems.

Discolored, stained or yellowed teeth can be made white again, chipped teeth made whole, spaces and gaps closed and slightly crooked teeth straightened.

Your smile can be customized into more ideal proportions in as little as two visits.

Invisalign

Many people are interested in straightening their teeth but don’t want to wear visible metal braces.

Invisalign is a  series of thin removable plastic trays called “aligners”. The patient wears the aligners until a more ideal smile is achieved. Perfect for adults who have had braces and just need a touch up or for people who have never had braces and want a minor correction. The aligners are undetectable and can be worn at all times.
